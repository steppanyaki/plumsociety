Julie Galich
Sales / Marketing
Julie is a marketing and media professional with over 18 years' experience helping clients define media objectives and delivering strategic marketing plans that drive sales. She has directed media, promotional, and marketing programs for Fortune 500 clients in a variety of industries including: QSR, fast casual, healthcare, consumer products, beverage, automotive, tourism and hospitality, and telecommunications.

Julie has successfully led cross-functional partners, advertising agencies, and franchisees to develop high performing national and local marketing programs. She has significant experience creating, testing, and launching new products and brands.

Julie is recognized as a media all-star by MediaWeek publication, received the Y&R "Rising Star" award, has earned the AT+T account leadership designation, was awarded the promotional planning and execution award for an account with billings in excess of $30 million in 2006, and received the Marketer of the Year award from Focus Brands in 2014.

Stacey Kirsch
product / distribution
Stacey is an experienced entrepreneur recognized for developing her own the first-to-market consumer footwear product, Flexflop. In this business she built a product from scratch, overseeing every component from design to manufacturing to marketing. She identified multiple ways to monetize the product, devising strategies that ultimately resulted in A-list celebrity endorsements and front-page press in celebrated magazines such as People, Life & Style, Self, Reader’s Digest. Stacey also appeared on The Big Idea "Minute to Millions" with Donny Deutsch. This behind-the-scenes perspective has not only sharpened her business acumen, but it’s helped her understand how to elevate and sustain a brand within multiple national and international markets. Stacey is also an award-winning marketer with a proven track record of creating unique sustainable brands from the ground up and driving their growth. She has worked on a variety of products including beauty, fashion, personal care, supplements, real-estate and technology. Her ability to target audiences has transformed sales and global reach for numerous companies. In her recent role at one of the fastest growing technology firms in the country, she was instrumental in growing a software brand from its beginning stages all the way through acquisition. As part of the companies' leadership, she identified ways to strengthen B2B relationships, redesign the partner program and establish partners in 27 countries. In this role she received the Channel Chiefs award and the Woman of Channel Award from CRN magazine.
