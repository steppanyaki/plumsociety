WHAT WE DO

Plum Society develops true partnerships with brands, designers, entrepreneurs, and category disruptors. We are on a mission to create, collaborate and bring one-of-a-kind products to market. While we work with a wide range of brands and consumer products, we continually seek out those that explore a new way of thinking; be it design, customer experience, or unique product attributes. Bringing ease, fun, and function to more people is in our DNA– it’s why we always strive to shine a light on the world’s bright ideas.

THE LIGHT

The goal of Plum Society is to seek out innovative brands wherever they may be... whether it's on a cocktail napkin or across the world. We are on a mission to create, collaborate, and bring to market products that revolutionize your everyday life.

THE SOCIETY

Team Plum is a society of enterprising female entrepreneurs with a passion for creative products. We are driven by seeing everyday ideas come to life; especially those with a focus on simplicity, style, and fun. The questions "why not?" and "how can we make things happen?" are always top of mind.

THE WHY

Our team has a track record of innovating, launching, and building successful brands. We are sales and marketing experts with a deep understanding of how to deliver a product to consumers. We leverage established relationships and multiple distribution channels to increase speed to market and success.

JULIE GALICH

sales / marketing

Julie is a marketing and media professional with over 18 years' experience helping clients define media objectives and delivering strategic marketing plans that drive sales. She has directed media, promotional, and marketing programs for Fortune 500 clients in a variety of industries including: QSR, fast casual, healthcare, consumer products, beverage, automotive, tourism and hospitality, and telecommunications.

Julie has successfully led cross-functional partners, advertising agencies, and franchisees to develop high performing national and local marketing programs. She has significant experience creating, testing, and launching new products and brands.

Julie is recognized as a media all-star by MediaWeek publication, received the Y&R "Rising Star " award, has earned the AT+T account leadership designation, was awarded the promotional planning and execution award for an account with billings in excess of $30 million in 2006, and received the Marketer of the Year award from Focus Brands in 2014.

❝ I launched a startup before it was cool because you get to be creative everyday. I am passionate about bringing new ideas to market and blazing a path with great products. ❞

STACEY KIRSCH

product / distribution

Stacey is an experienced entrepreneur recognized for developing her own first-to-market consumer footwear product, Flexflop. This product, which Stacey singly brought from concept to design to production, garnered A-list celebrity endorsements and was featured on major magazines such as People, Life & Style, Self, Reader’s Digest. Stacey also appeared on The Big Idea "Minute to Millions " with Donny Deutsch.

Stacey is also an award-winning marketer with a proven track record of building high-growth brands from the ground up and has worked on a variety of product categories, including beauty, fashion, personal care, supplements, real-estate and technology. In her recent role at one of the fastest growing technology firms in the country, she was instrumental in growing a software brand from its beginning stages, launching it in 27 countries and guiding it through acquisition. She received the Channel Chiefs award and the Woman of Channel Award from CRN magazine.

❝ I like to think of myself as a builder at heart. I always get excited about the opportunity to build something from the ground up, working on all aspects of a business, and getting my hands dirty. I'm most inspired and energized by great innovation and style. ❞

STEPHANIE HOUNG

operations / ecommerce / tech

Stephanie started out as a professional voice artist in Asia and eventually built her personal brand into a media marketing consultancy, working with the likes of Disney and Toyota. Stephanie has an MBA degree at Cornell University, which she used as a launch pad to discover her business interests. More recently, Stephanie has consulted on sourcing, operations, and logistics for numerous successful ecommerce businesses.

The range of experience from the divergent paths Stephanie has taken eventually culminated into her getting involved with helping multiple startups from the seed stage, where her ability to wear many hats and leverage all her skills has also become her passion. Stephanie is also a self-taught coder and is always looking for more opportunities to tap into her international experience and abilities to help build and realize successful enterprises.
