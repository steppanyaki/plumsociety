Nice to meet you

Julie Galich
Sales / Marketing
Julie is a marketing and media professional with over 18 years' experience interpreting a client's marketing goals to define media objectives and strategies, developing and recommending communications plans, managing budgets, and delivering marketing plans that will actively engage consumers and drive sales. She has directed and strategically led media, promotional, and marketing programs for Fortune 500 clients in a variety of industries including: QSR, fast casual, healthcare, consumer products, beverage, automotive, tourism and hospitality, and telecommunications. Julie is a collaborative leader that has managed and influenced cross functional partners, advertising agencies and franchisees to develop highly successful national and local marketing programs. She has significant experience creating, testing and launching new products and brands with integrated marketing campaigns leveraging both traditional and emerging media. Recognized as a media all-star by MediaWeek publication, received the Y&R "Rising Star" award, has earned the AT+T account leadership designation, was awarded the promotional planning and execution award for an account with billings in excess of $30 million in 2006, and received the Marketer of the Year award from Focus Brands in 2014.

Stacey Kirsch
product / distribution
Stacey is an entrepreneur skilled at innovating brand strategies for a wide range of products, including her own pioneering product, FlexFlop. She developed the first footwear product of its kind overseeing all aspects from design to manufacturing and marketing. She identified multiple ways to monetize the business, devising strategies that ultimately resulted in A-list celebrity endorsements, front-page press and television appearances. This behind-the-scenes perspective has helped her understand how to elevate and sustain a brand within multiple national and international markets.
